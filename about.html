About This Website:

I made this website because I wanted a way to express my feelings about music in a way that many people could see. I have some very strong ways that I feel about music and I have always wanted to express them in writing. I specialize in electronic music, since I was little I have always liked the electronic beats of pop music. But I always despised the lyrics, it wasn't until my dad showed me an electronic band that he liked when I discovered the genre. I have loved it ever since.

Flash forward to when I was assigned to make a website with Html/CSS and I thought to make a review site for my favorite music. I strive to express my feelings about music through the form of this website. I decided to go for a more retro look for my website; enjoy!
