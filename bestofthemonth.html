I absolutely love this song, and I really appreciate the work that went into making this song. It has so many sounds happening at once and is perfect in every way. This song was the perfect blend of the old timey sound effects of Link to the Past and the new sounds that have progressed since the original Link to The Past came out. It has many sounds that make it sound almost exactly like Link to The Past but it has many new sounds and it really puts a spin on the old game's soundtrack.

This song was interesting, as someone perfectly describes it in the comments "your songs are great, right when somebody would click off, the whole thing explodes with sound." Meganeko has an iteresting style, and it can only be described with one word: energy. All of Meganeko's songs have a unique energy that has a vibrant positive sound. This song has a very modern feel that is absolutely astonishing.

This song was engaging, it has a harsh sound and good samples, I like how it sounds in general. I love the melody in the beginning of the song. It gives the whole song a whole new flavor than that of the rest of the song. I also love the alarm sound effect in some parts, it may be hard to hear in some sections of the song but it adds some texture to the song.

Many know the true intensity of a Meganeko song, because Meganeko can make a song unlike any other. It's fascinating how Meganeko can turn any song into a masterpiece. This song has an 8-bit theme to start off with but it explodes into a hard hitting song that is incredible. There are certain sections that are just perfect in this song. I absolutely love Meganeko's style of music and I especially love this song.
